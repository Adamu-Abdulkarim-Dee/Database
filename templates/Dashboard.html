Ponda Database

Pondadb is a web-based database system that allows primary and secondary schools to store their student information in one place with an easy-to-use interface. Administrator can easily access the database at anytime.
We help you to improve the learning process by giving you peace of mind, which is an important factor in achieving a sucesseful education.

Why you should use pondadb

Ponda database is used to store student information. It helps the schools to organize their data and keep it securely on web.

you will never forget a student because you have the right to know who they are. This database keeps your student information safe at all the time.

you can search and retrive the student information by visiting their albums. School should use ponda database because it helps to reduce the risk of data loss and helps schools to stay update about their student history and their performance.

In this didgital age, school should consider using ponda database to store the necessary data of their student such as names, year of graduation, data of birth, student address, guardians information, and grade.
