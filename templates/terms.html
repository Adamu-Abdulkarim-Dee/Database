2. Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on user devices by a website. They are used to help website to remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language, the page you visited, and any preferences or settings you may have selected. Cookies help to make your browsing experience more personalize.

There are two main types of cookies:
session cookies and persistent cookies. Session cookies are temporary files that are deleted when you close your browser, while persistent cookies are stored on your devices for a longer period of time, even after you close your browser.

Our website use both session and persistent to provide you with a more good browsing experience. For example, we may use persistent cookies to remember your preferences during your visit, or we may use persistent cookies to remember your preferences for future visits.
